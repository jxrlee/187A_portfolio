1year shoot

July 2009 at the Heritage Rose Garden in San Jose

Photographed and directed by Joseph Lee.


This photo shoot was a gift to my best friends, Patrick and Rebecca, to celebrate their one year anniversary. The concept of the 1year shoot was an outdoor play date. I told Patrick and Rebecca to come dressed up and ready to step out of their comfort zones and this is the result! Their decision to wear white and red complemented the surroundings incredibly well.
