NomSpot

December 2010

Designed by Joseph Lee.

NomSpot is a location-based daily deals platform for food and social media platform for venues. It allows restaurants to post daily deals and interact with their customers through their venue page. NomSpot's aim was to become the premier site for venue-customer interaction and daily deals.

Logo Design I

Before designing the logo, there were a few requirements that I wanted to accomplish. The app itself was meant to be used by college students and so the logo must appeal to that target audience. I wanted people to think of NomSpot as both a noun and a verb (1. a nomspot/place and 2. to spot food)

Logo Design II

After several redesigns, I arrived at the logo above but there was one more requirement I had achieved. I wanted to make NomSpot something that's distinct and instantly recognizable, like Yelp's vinyl decals on store windows. The end result is a maroon background that will be universal to the logo.

Landing Page II
